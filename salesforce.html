Salesforce implementation and customization

We are at ‘Alcor Engineering’ team of experts that can tailor Salesforce to align with your company's branding, workflows, and processes. We can set up integrations with your other systems, promoting seamless collaboration among your team and maximising the benefits of your Salesforce investment.


Salesforce configuration involves using point-and-click tools to make changes to applications, such as modifying the user interface, creating reports, workflows, and dashboards. On the other hand, Salesforce customization entails incorporating advanced business processes, logic, and calculations into Salesforce applications through coding expertise.
